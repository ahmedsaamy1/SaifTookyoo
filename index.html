Saif Tokyo

About the Artist

Saif Tokyo is a renowned Egyptian rapper from Meet Ghamr, Egypt. He began his illustrious music career in 2018 and has since risen to fame with his unique style, powerful lyrics, and captivating performances. Known for his ability to blend traditional Egyptian sounds with modern rap, Saif Tokyo has become a symbol of innovation and creativity in the music industry.
